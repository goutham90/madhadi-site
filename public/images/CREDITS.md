### Honesty notes on subject fit
- **Manufacturing/bioreactors**: the two manufacturing shots show cleanroom workers /
  protective gear rather than literal stainless bioreactor vessels. Strong industrial
  pharma feel, but no dedicated bioreactor close-up.
- **Analytical instruments**: these are generic lab-bench / lab-machine shots; none is a
  verified, labeled HPLC or chromatography stack specifically.
- **Records/server**: `records-server.jpg` is network cabling / racks (data-center feel),
  which reads as "data integrity / archives" rather than physical paper archives.
- No US-gov direct downloads (NCI Visuals Online / NIAID Flickr / FDA / CDC PHIL) were
  used as primary sources; the two NCI images arrived via their Unsplash mirror. Unsplash
  was used throughout because it provided reliable, working direct-download URLs at the
  target size.
